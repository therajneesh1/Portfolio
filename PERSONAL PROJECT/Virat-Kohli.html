About

Virat Kohli, widely regarded as one of the modern-day cricketing legends, has left an indelible mark on the sport with his unparalleled skill, unwavering determination, and charismatic leadership. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli's cricketing journey began at a young age, and he swiftly rose through the ranks to become the captain of the Indian national cricket team. Known for his aggressive batting style, Kohli has amassed an impressive record of centuries across all formats, making him one of the most prolific run-scorers in the history of the game. Beyond his remarkable on-field achievements, Kohli is admired for his commitment to fitness and his passion for the sport, setting new standards for excellence. As a captain, he has led India to numerous victories, including historic series wins on foreign soil. Off the field, Virat Kohli's philanthropic efforts and advocacy for social causes showcase his commitment to making a positive impact beyond the boundary. A true cricketing icon, Kohli's journey is a testament to his talent, resilience, and enduring love for the game.
